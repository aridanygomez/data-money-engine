Frequently Asked Questions

How do the alerts work?

After subscribing, you register your Slack or Discord webhook URL via our API. Every morning the bot scrapes prices from 30+ LLM providers. If any price changes by more than your threshold (default 5%), your webhook gets an instant notification.

Which models are tracked?

We track GPT-4o, GPT-4 Turbo, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Claude 3 Opus, Gemini 1.5 Pro/Flash, Llama 3.1 (via Groq, Together, Fireworks), Mistral Large, DeepSeek V3, Command R+, and 20+ more. New models are added automatically.

What does the REST API include (Pro)?

The Pro API gives you GET /api/v1/models for current prices, GET /api/v1/cheapest?n=10 for the cheapest options, and GET /api/v1/history/:id for 90-day price history. Perfect for building dashboards or cost-optimization scripts.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from the Stripe portal at any time. Your access continues until the end of the billing period. No questions asked.

Is there a free tier?

The public site at llm-pricing.dev shows current prices for free (up to 20 models). Paid plans add alerts and deeper API access.
